Tu Jhoothi Main Makkaar

Tu Jhoothi Main Makkaar abbreviated as TJMM, is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language romantic comedy film directed by Luv Ranjan and written by Rahul Mody, Luv Ranjan. Produced by Luv Films and T-Series Films, it stars Ranbir Kapoor and Shraddha Kapoor, alongside Dimple Kapadia and Anubhav Singh Bassi in key supporting roles. The film received mixed reviews from critics.
